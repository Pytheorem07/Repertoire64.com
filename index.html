How to Use

1. Repertoires: Open the Repertoires menu (top right) to create and manage multiple repertoires.

2. Build & Annotate: Play moves to build lines. Add names and comments to positions. Click Stockfish moves to play them.

3. Smart Training: The trainer remembers lines you've trained. "Smart Skip" auto-plays the start of branching lines to save you time.

4. Drag & Drop: You can click or drag pieces naturally. Turn on Legal Dots in Settings if you want hints.

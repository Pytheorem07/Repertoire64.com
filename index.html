How to Use Repertoire64

1. Build Your Openings

Click and drag pieces (or simply click the destination square) to record moves. Under the Build tab, write variation names and comments for any move. Add as many branching alternative moves as you want!

2. ChessDB Auto-Build & Repertoire Crafter

Harness the power of the public ChessDB master games database to auto-generate lines in seconds. Build a brand new complete theoretical opening tree using the ChessDB Repertoire Crafter in the repertoires menu (matching Aggressive, Positional, or Out of Book styles), or recursively expand any existing line directly from the board!

3. Deep Engine & DB Analysis

Stockfish calculates top-tier move options in Standard Algebraic Notation (e.g. e4 or Nf3) alongside evaluation metrics. Click any engine path to automatically execute that sequence on the board. ChessDB query logs keep you aligned with optimal master theory.

4. Spaced repetition & Repertoire Tour

Launch customized study lines in the Train tab. By default, the program auto-plays opponent actions. Use the new Watch Rep button below the chessboard to automatically sit back and watch your whole repertoire played out beautifully branch-by-branch!

5. Deviation Analysis

Under the Games tab, input your Lichess or Chess.com username. The application downloads and scans recent games, pinpointing exactly where your play strayed from your documented preparation, allowing you to instantly add those lines to your repertoire with a single click.
